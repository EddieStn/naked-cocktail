Classic Cocktails

Below are a list of the most popular cocktails our expert bartenders choose from! We choose cocktails that compliment each other perfectly!

Apple Martini

Originally called an Adam's Apple Martini, it was created in 1996 at Lola's West Hollywood restaurant.

Cosmopolitan

Gained popularity in the 1990's on the television programme 'Sex and the City' where it was the drink of choice for Sarah Jessica Parker and her girlfriends!

Elderflower Cooler

Thirst-quenching and refreshing, this is the perfect drink to enjoy on a hot summer's day (or a cold summer's day as is often the case in the UK!).

Espresso Martini

Created in the early 1980's by bartender Dick Bradsell. According to Dick, a famous model entered the Soho Brasserie where he was working and asked him to create a drink that would "Wake me up!"

CLASSIC MOJITO

One of the oldest cocktails around, the Mojito was born in Havana, Cuba, and has many variations, including the popular Strawberry Mojito.

AMARETTO SOURS

Sweet & Sour - you'll find this drink hard to put down!

SEX ON THE BEACH

A hen party favourite, named after the two things people love to do in Florida!

TOM COLLINS

Dating back to the late 1800's, this was a favourite amongst many young professionals in the bar rooms of New York City.

CHOCOLATE MARTINI

Chocolate and alcohol...need we say more? Indulge in this great tasting classic cocktail

MOCKTAILS

Naked Range

We released the Naked Range due to increased demand for a healthy cocktail. Our exclusive 'Naked Range' replaces the artificial juices and sugars with only 100% pure, natural and fresh ingredients. We will be up and about early on the day of your party picking the finest ingredients from our local green grocer. We choose cocktails for your party that compliment each other perfectly!

Blackberry Ocean Breeze

This Vodka based fruity delight will be certain to get your tastebuds tingling. With it's high anti-oxidant levels, this brain boosting cocktail will put you in good stead for our cocktail quiz!

Blueberry Lemon Breeze

Combining Gin with the natural sweetness of blueberries and bitterness of lemon juice, (Oh and did we mention prosecco!) this superfood cocktail will leave your skin feeling great!

Blueberry Mint Cosmo

This healthy twist on a classic cocktail will leave your palate cleansed and wanting more!

Raspberry Gin Sour

This very trendy cocktail is the most popular in our Naked range. Exceedingly tasty and easy on the eye, take our word - you will love it!

Gin Garden

This simple, but unbelievably fresh and tasty gin based cocktail is full of antioxidants.

Strawberry Mojito

A fresh and tasty alternative to the classic Mojito. This vibrant and colourful cocktail will look great on Instagram! Just remember to #NakedCocktails
